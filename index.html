Our Journey

Development Timeline

Our path to the ultimate AI study companion.

1
Month 1

System Optimization & Workflow Synergy

  • Conduct rigorous internal testing to ensure the “1+1>2” compounding synergy is functioning seamlessly (e.g., verifying that the auto-grading engine accurately feeds data into the podcast/quiz generation).
  • Refine LLM prompts for maximum accuracy and reduce latency for iPadOS Apple Pencil interactions.
2
Month 2

Advanced Feature Finalization

  • Finalize the development of the high-complexity features: the Format-Cloning Mock Paper generation and the predictive analytics study planner.
3
Month 3

Closed Alpha Testing & Focus Groups

  • Deploy the working build to a small, controlled group of Alpha testers (15–20 students).
  • Monitor the accuracy of the “Personal Study Code” mapping and gather qualitative feedback on the proactive AI interventions.
4
Month 4

Comprehensive Beta Testing & UX Polishing

  • Expand to a Closed Beta with 100 target high school and undergraduate students.
  • Stress-test the system under real-world, daily study loads. Finalize the UI/UX based on direct student feedback to ensure a zero-friction, native iPad experience.
5
Month 5

App Store Submission & Pre-Launch Marketing

  • Submit Vibenotes to the Apple App Store and navigate the review process.
  • Finalize the freemium subscription tiers and execute the initial marketing campaign, heavily leveraging student ambassadors and social media to demonstrate the “autonomous workflow.”
6
Month 6

Official Public Launch & Iterative Growth

  • Execute the App Store launch and begin aggressive user acquisition.
  • Monitor initial system stability, gather the first wave of post-launch user analytics, and begin scoping minor iterative updates based on live user data.
